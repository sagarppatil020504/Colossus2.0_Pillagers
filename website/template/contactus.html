Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment line at +1 (555) 987-6543, using our online patient portal, or by visiting our reception desk in person. For specialized departments, we recommend calling ahead to check availability.

What insurance plans do you accept?

Medicare Hospital accepts most major insurance plans, including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and United Healthcare. For specific coverage questions, please contact our billing department or your insurance provider.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical records if applicable, and any referral forms. Arriving 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time is recommended for registration.

Are visitors allowed in the hospital?

Yes, visitors are welcome during visiting hours from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. For intensive care units and special departments, specific visiting restrictions may apply. Please check with the nursing station for details.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our secure online patient portal. Alternatively, you can submit a written request to our Medical Records Department. Allow 3-5 business days for processing.
