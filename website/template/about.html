About Medicare

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare Since 1985

Our Story

Providing Quality Healthcare for Over 38 Years

Medicare Hospital was founded in 1985 with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to our community. What began as a small clinic with just 15 dedicated professionals has grown into a comprehensive medical center with state-of-the-art facilities and over 200 healthcare specialists.

Over the decades, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing healthcare needs of our community while staying true to our founding principles of compassion, excellence, and patient-centered care.

Today, Medicare Hospital stands as a beacon of healthcare excellence, serving thousands of patients annually with comprehensive medical services across dozens of specialties. Our commitment to innovation, research, and continuous improvement has established us as a trusted leader in modern healthcare delivery.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To enhance the health and wellbeing of our community by delivering exceptional, compassionate care that is accessible to all. We are committed to clinical excellence, patient safety, and continuous innovation in medical practice.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider, renowned for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and compassionate patient care. We strive to set new standards in healthcare delivery while making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.
